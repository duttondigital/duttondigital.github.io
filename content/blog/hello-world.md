This is the first post on the Dutton Digital blog. We'll be sharing updates about our products, tips for local businesses, and thoughts on keeping technology simple.

## Why we started this blog

Running a local business is hard enough without wrestling with complicated tech. We built Dutton Digital to make things easier, and this blog is part of that mission.

Here's what you can expect:

- Product updates and new features
- Practical tips for getting online
- Honest takes on what works (and what doesn't)

## Simple by design

Just like our products, this blog is deliberately simple. No comments section, no tracking, no cookies. Just words on a page.
